Syndal Gardens is a new apartment development in preconstruction at 37-39 Hunter Street, Glen Waverley, just minutes walk from Syndal train station. This 3 storey building is planned with underground parking. 1-, 2- and 3-bedroom apartments are coming soon.

Location matters

Residents will enjoy easy access to Syndal train station, bus stops, Monash and Eastlink Freeways to reach Melbourne, shopping centers at Box Hill, Chadstone, the Knox and the Glen. The population of Monash City is very diverse and multi cultural. The place is offering a variety of cuisine. Syndal is surrounded by public parks with tennis courts, walking and bike paths. Monash Aquatic and Recreational Centre is a modern facility offerring group fitness classes and programmes for children. For some fun at night you are welcome to go to a bar with karaoke, bowling, a cinema and much more.

The presence of Wesley college, Glen Waverley Secondary College, Mount Waverley Secondary College, Holmesglen Institute, Monash University offers a lot of opportunities for education and skills improvement.
