Enjoy living at Syndal Gardens

Syndal Gardens is a new apartment development, located in one of Glen Waverley`s most desirable area, minutes walk from train station. It provides the ultimate in essential apartment living surrounded by low-maintenance, unbeatable lifestyle convenience.

Syndal Gardens is a 3-storey building with underground parking. 1, 2 & 3-bedroom apartments with liveable floor plans, open interiors and a private balcony.


Contemporary design and carefully considered spaces offer optimal functional living for the modern lifestyle.

A warm neutral colour palette of interior is complimented with solid European Oak flooring creating a welcoming and calm environment.

The kitchen is fitted with quality stainless steel appliances and reconstructed bench-tops adding subtle textures.

Location matters

Residents will enjoy access to Syndal train station, bus stops, Monach and Eastlink Freeways to reach Melbourne, shopping centers at Box Hill, Chadstone, the Knox and The Glen.

Syndal is surrounded by public parks with tennis court, walking and bike paths. Monash Aquatic and Recreational Centre is a modern facility offering fitness classes and programmes for children. For some fun welcome to karaoke bar, bowling, cinema and restaurants.

The presents of Wesley college, Glen Waverley Secondary College, Mount Waverley Secondary college, Holmesglen Institute, Monach University offers education opportunities and skills improvement.
